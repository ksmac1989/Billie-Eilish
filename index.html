O'Connell was born in Los Angeles, California, on December 18, 2001. She is the daughter of Maggie Baird and Patrick O’Connell. Eilish is of Irish and Scottish descent. She was raised in Los Angeles' Highland Park. She was homeschooled her whole life, and joined the Los Angeles Children's Chorus at age eight. Eilish said she has been singing since childhood and began writing songs at age 11, taking after her elder brother Finneas O'Connell who was already writing, performing and producing his own songs with his band.

Eilish and her brother Finneas, who produces most of her musics, collaborate on songwriting. She said Finneas, who also performs with her on live shows, is her partner in making music. Eilish and her brother "like to completely make up things and become characters" and "have songs that are really fictional”. Eilish said a number of the songs they write also derive from her experiences: "A lot of the time we go through the same stuff, so it's a mixture of what he's going through and what I'm going through.
