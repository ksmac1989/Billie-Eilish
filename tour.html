Eilish will take those new tunes to the fans with an upcoming world tour–dubbed "When We Fall Asleep"
that stretches from February to August. Her stateside fans will get a chance to see her starting at Coachella

After that, she’ll tour the west coast with stopes at Portland’s Moda Center and Redmond’s Marymoor Park.
